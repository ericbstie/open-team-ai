# Two-tier directive authority

Meta-agent directives split into two tiers. Mechanical directives (tune effective
parallelism within the CLI caps, sleep/wake an idle agent) are applied directly by
the runtime and logged. Judgment directives (respecialize, rebalance a team,
reallocate a task) are delivered to the orchestrator as high-priority context; it
must act or explicitly decline with a reason, and declines are logged events the
meta-agent sees. All-binding was rejected — two brains, one body, and they fight.
All-advisory was rejected — it makes the meta layer decorative. Two-tier keeps one
commander with an empowered inspector.
